Rings Vs Blings

  • Survive waves of blings.
  • You control rings. Rings shoot at the nearest blings.
  • Stay away from blings! They explode on impact or death.
  • Select units by selecting an area.
  • Right click after selecting units to move.
  • Press t to go berserk.
  • Press h while moving to stop.
